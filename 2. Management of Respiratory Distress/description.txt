This course will offer a review of oxygenation and ventilation, respiratory failure, oxygen delivery systems as well as the appropriate resources for treating various respiratory conditions, all of which are crucial elements in understanding the human respiratory system and the management of respiratory decompensation and failure. 

The course consists of 6 chapters, with 15 articles and 41 questions in total. For the chapters that have questions, we will ask an array of questions to assess your skills and identify existing knowledge gaps before going into the theory.
